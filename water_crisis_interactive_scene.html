Water Crisis in Chennai

It is clear from the previous two graphs that the current reservoir system is not working. The situation has reached a crisis level. Though the state of Karnataka has been helping, the help has been varying. Here is the chart where you can analyze how different reservoirs have performed yearly with respect to rainfall even after receiving help from the State of Karnataka. With little analysis, you will find that the help received is not enough! The government must find other ways of dealing with this crisis. Few of the things that can be done are:

  • Rainwater harvesting in residential buildings
  • Spreading more awareness regarding the ways for saving water
  • Getting more river water released from the State of Karnataka
